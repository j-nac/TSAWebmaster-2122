About

Created in 2012, we started with the goal of organizing and supporting local artists and shows. In 2021 after a year off due to the pandemic, our organization went online to keep things safe; yet we stay determined to help everybody continue to enjoy music. Our goal here for the ArchFest Music Festival is for people to enjoy, have access, and build a community to support as much as possible.

Special Thanks

Thanks and appreciation goes to Holly Anderson who started this organization to support our local community; Slade Grayson, our Editor-in-Chief and Co-founder who supported Holly every step of the way; and last but not least, Emily Ziao, who is our networker and we wouldn't have made it this far without her.
